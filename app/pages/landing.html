WatStock is bringing to market an AI self-service platform to give retail investors and small-medium sized institutional clients the ability to use WatStock’s predictions to generate results.

WatStock is employing a custom implementation of Natural Language Processing (NLP) alongside its proprietary Extreme Learning Machine (ELM) to make accurate security price & market trend predictions.
